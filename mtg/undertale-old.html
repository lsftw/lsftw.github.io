Undertale

Colorless = Monsters / Blank-slate Humans
Colorless is generally stronger, but there are many colorless hosers.

Lot of removal/steal effects to deal with lords.
Lot of indestructibility effects, but almost no graveyard recursion.

Designed for draft, archetypes:
White = Colorless lords, mercy
Blue = ETB recursion
Black = -1/-1 counter manipulation
Red = Aggressive, become all colors synergy
Green = Indestructibility, primary human color
